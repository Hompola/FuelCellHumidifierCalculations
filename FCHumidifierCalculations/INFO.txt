Try opening the DataSets directory and filling it with .txt files that have tables of values to interpolate between! 
Use text headers for each column! 
You may also use a list format, with the headers breaking up each column.

You have a couple of examples in your DataSets folder already, if the programme made your directory. 
Otherwise, with an empty DataSets folder, run the programme again to get some examples!
Both DataSet1 and DataSet2 contain the Physical Properties of Dry Air at 1 Bar Pressure
